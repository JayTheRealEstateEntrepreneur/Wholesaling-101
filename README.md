Get Rich Guide 101


A beginner's roadmap into real estate wholesaling — built by someone learning the field, for people just starting out.




Why This Exists

Most people hear "wholesaling" and picture two things: a get-rich-quick scheme, or a legit path into real estate with no money down. The truth is somewhere in between — it's a real strategy, with real rules, real risk, and a real learning curve.

This guide exists because that learning curve is steep when you're figuring it out alone. No one hands beginners a map. You find out what a contract assignment is after you've already messed one up. You learn about disclosure laws after a deal falls through. Get Rich Guide 101 is an attempt to hand new wholesalers that map before they need it — not after.

Who This Is For

Beginners. If you already know what an assignment fee is, this probably isn't for you. If you're wondering whether wholesaling is a real business or a scam you saw on YouTube, start here.

A Quick Honest Note

Wholesaling laws and licensing requirements vary by state, and some jurisdictions have tightened rules around contract assignments in recent years. This guide is educational, not legal advice — always verify current requirements in your area before doing deals.
